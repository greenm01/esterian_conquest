ECMAINT RandSeed Capture (COM-stub method)
==================================================

READSEED.COM reads 4 bytes from 3529:03A6 (Borland Pascal RandSeed)
and writes them to SEED.BIN.

PRNG: RandSeed = RandSeed * 0x08088405 + 1 (Borland Pascal LCG)
